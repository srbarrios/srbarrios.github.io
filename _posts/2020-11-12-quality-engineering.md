As I explained in a previous post, my team at SUSE uses BDD methodology, at least in theory.

I wanted to introduce you again to the concept of QA, and the difference between Quality Assurance and Quality Assistance (it is also often called Quality Engineering).

I think this is a very interesting talk for the teams, including the QA team itself, as some teams still have the idea that a QA Engineer is in charge of testing the product so that it is delivered without defects, but it is a lot more than that!
